
# 📊 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)


## 📌Project Overview

This project features a comprehensive Power BI Dashboard designed to analyze the sales performance of Blinkit, India’s leading Quick Commerce platform. The goal was to take raw, messy retail data and transform it into actionable business insights regarding outlet efficiency, customer preferences, and inventory distribution.

As a student project, this focuses on the intersection of Data Analytics and Business Intelligence, simulating how a data analyst would report to a regional manager to optimize sales.

## 📌 Executive Summary
In the high-stakes **Quick Commerce (Q-Commerce)** industry, understanding customer behavior and outlet efficiency is the difference between profit and loss. This project analyzes **$1.20M in sales data** for Blinkit to identify trends in consumer preferences and outlet performance across different Indian city tiers.

This project demonstrates the transition from raw retail data to actionable **Business Intelligence**.


## 🛠️ The Business Challenge
Blinkit operates a massive network of "Dark Stores." To optimize growth, I addressed the following key business questions:
1. **Outlet Efficiency:** Which outlet size (Small, Medium, Large) provides the highest sales volume?
2. **Consumer Health Trends:** How does the demand for "Low Fat" vs. "Regular" products vary by location?
3. **Geographic Growth:** Which city tiers (Tier 1, 2, or 3) are driving the most revenue?
4. **Maturity Analysis:** Does the age of an outlet (Establishment Year) correlate with higher customer ratings?



## 🚀 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Revenue:** $1.20M
* **Average Transaction Value:** $141
* **Total Items Sold:** 8,523
* **Average Customer Rating:** 3.9 / 5.0



## 🧠 Technical Workflow
### 1. Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
* Handled missing values in `Item Weight` and `Outlet Size`.
* Standardized "Fat Content" labels (mapping "LF" and "low fat" to "Low Fat").
* Cleaned and categorized `Item Type` for better segmentation.

### 2. Data Modeling & DAX
* Developed custom **DAX Measures** for dynamic calculations of Total Sales, Average Sales, and Rating counts.
* Established relationships between tables to allow for seamless cross-filtering.

### 3. Data Visualization
* **Funnel Chart:** Used to track sales across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities.
* **Donut Charts:** Visualizing market share of Fat Content and Outlet Sizes.
* **Line Chart:** Identifying historical sales trends based on the year of establishment.

## 💡 Real-World Insights
* **Tier 3 Opportunity:** Tier 3 cities contributed significantly to total sales, suggesting that Q-Commerce has deep penetration beyond just metros.
* **Size Matters:** Medium-sized outlets consistently outperformed both Small and Large outlets in terms of sales-to-space efficiency.
* **Health Consciousness:** Low-fat products dominate the "Snacks" and "Dairy" categories, indicating a health-conscious customer base.
